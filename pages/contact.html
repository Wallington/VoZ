What is this game?

Visions of Zosimos is a game that combines a collectible card game and turn-based tactical tabletop wargame while also harnessing the social aspects of a massively multi-player online game. The story brings players in as students of the mystic traditions of Hermetic Alchemy and Gnosticism; their actions will discover the hidden secrets of our world while battling for the fate of our universe.

Which operating systems run VoZ? What game engine do we use?

As of right now, VoZ is available for Windows only. For now, we don’t support Mac or Linux. The game engine we use is HeroEngine.

Is there a physical version of the game?

Not at this time, but we do prototyping with physical cards and hex maps from time to time to test new cards. We’re looking into the viability of printing physical cards, either for promotions or as a stand-alone version of the game.

Do I have to pay to get the good stuff?

No! Paying can expand the different types of cards at your disposal, but we’ve worked hard to make sure that the free cards are just as good and can hold their own in any match. In fact, there are some things that you can only do with free card sets that you can’t do with paid sets!

How do I play? Where can I get an Alpha Key?

Send an email to contact@foreverinteractive.com to request an Alpha Key. While you’re downloading the game, make sure to check out our tutorials on YouTube, or read through our Alpha Prototype tutorial!
